Strategic Business Findings

B2B Strategy

Corporate & Government Focus

Evidence

Wholesale, Government, and Corporate segments each drive ~4B in revenue. B2B dominates the revenue mix.

Impact

Heavy reliance on institutional contracts. Retail is secondary but provides stability during contract gaps.

Action

Launch B2B Key Account Management (KAM) program with custom credit terms and dedicated support.

Product Mix

Market Demand Uniformity

Evidence

Balanced demand across all categories (~5.6B each). No single category dominates — diversified revenue profile.

Impact

Low concentration risk but complex SKU management. Opportunity for cross-sell bundling strategies.

Action

Implement cross-selling bundles (e.g., Tea + Snacks) to increase average ticket without marketing overhead.

Regional

Geographic Expansion Opportunity

Evidence

Stable, consistent performance across Brazil, Colombia, and Mexico with no regional outliers.

Impact

Proven market model works across LATAM. Multi-country logistics increase costs by 2-4%.

Action

Centralize a Regional Distribution Hub to consolidate supply chain and reduce shipping overhead.
